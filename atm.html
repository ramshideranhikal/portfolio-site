ATM Re-imagined

June'17 | Sketch | Illustrator

An imaginative app which can connect to your ATM card. Once you save the ATM card details with the app, you can enter the withdrawal amount and PIN securely in your phone to withdraw money from ATM machine, the app will send this data to bank's server and when you insert your card, the ATM machine will connect with server and withdraw the money instantly as per your request in the app.
