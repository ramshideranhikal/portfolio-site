#SpacedChallenge

Jan'18 | Sketch | Photoshop | Principle

#spacedChallenge was a contest hosted by designer Dann Petty in YouTube and Dribbble. "The SPACED challenge was about designing either a logo, a website, or an app for a fictional space travel company that takes passengers to different destinations in our galaxy in one day, and back to Earth safely."

The challenge was a good exercise for me to explore more on the interaction side. I created a mobile app and tried to simplify the booking process within few clicks.
